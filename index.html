History & Heritage

Our Rich History

Founded in 1972, PI SIGMA has established itself as a beacon of excellence in brotherhood and leadership. Our fraternity has grown from humble beginnings to become a respected institution across multiple chapters nationwide.

Through decades of dedication to our core values, we have shaped countless leaders and fostered lifelong bonds among our members.

Leadership Excellence

Developing Tomorrow's Leaders

At PI SIGMA, leadership development is at the core of our mission. We provide comprehensive training programs, mentorship opportunities, and hands-on experience in organizational management.

Our members graduate not just with degrees, but with the skills and confidence to lead in their chosen fields.

Community Impact

Making a Difference

Service to our community is a fundamental aspect of PI SIGMA's identity. Our members contribute thousands of volunteer hours annually through various initiatives and partnerships with local organizations.

From food drives to educational programs, we're committed to creating positive change in our communities.

Brotherhood Bond

Lifelong Brotherhood

The bonds formed within PI SIGMA extend far beyond college years. Our brotherhood creates a network of support, friendship, and professional connections that last a lifetime.

Through our various events and activities, we strengthen these bonds and create memories that will last forever.
