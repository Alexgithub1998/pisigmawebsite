History

Our Legacy

Founded in 1972 at a time of great social change, PI SIGMA emerged as a fraternity committed to excellence and brotherhood. From its humble beginnings with just 12 founding members, the fraternity has grown to over 50 chapters nationwide.

Through the decades, PI SIGMA has maintained its commitment to academic excellence, leadership development, and community service while adapting to meet the changing needs of each new generation.
